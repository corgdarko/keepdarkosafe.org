Darko

The corgi with a boss-level personality

Hello. If you’re reading this, you’re already part of the club — people who instantly fall for Darko. He’s not “just a dog”… he’s a corgi with attitude, charisma, drama, and a huge heart.

Darko has that rare “small but powerful” energy. He walks like the floor belongs to him, and when he enters a room, the vibe changes instantly: either he looks at you like a judge, or greets you like you’ve been best friends forever.

His personality is the perfect mix: curious (he needs to inspect EVERYTHING), protective (even if he’s only about a foot tall), professional clown (unexpected zoomies), and master negotiator (he can get snacks with just one look).

The funniest part is his “corgi mode”: out of nowhere he activates turbo, spins around like a top, and then collapses as if he just worked a 12-hour shift. And yes… if you hear a dramatic sigh, it’s probably Darko not getting attention in the last 30 seconds.

With love,
— Darko the Corgi
